Day-Wise Itinerary

1

Day 1: Rishikesh to Govindghat

Your journey begins with an early morning departure from Rishikesh, driving alongside the scenic Alaknanda River. Passing through Devprayag, Rudraprayag, and Joshimath, the changing landscapes make the long drive truly mesmerizing. Arrive at Govindghat by evening.

Stay: Hotel in Govindghat  |  Meals: Dinner
2

Day 2: Govindghat to Pulna to Ghangaria

After breakfast, take a short jeep ride to Pulna, the starting point of your trek. From here, begin your ascent through lush forests, walking alongside the beautiful Pushpawati River. Reach Ghangaria, a peaceful mountain village, by evening.

Stay: Hotel / Homestay  |  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
3

Day 3: Ghangaria to Valley of Flowers and Back

Explore the magical Valley of Flowers, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its vibrant alpine blooms. The trek is around 8 km one way, taking you through a colorful landscape filled with rare flowers like Blue Poppy, Cobra Lily, and orchids.

Stay: Hotel / Homestay  |  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
4

Day 4: Ghangaria to Hemkund Sahib and Back

Early in the morning, begin a steep yet rewarding trek towards Hemkund Sahib, covering approximately 6 km one way. Climb higher until you reach the sacred Gurudwara beside a serene glacial lake.

Stay: Hotel / Homestay  |  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
5

Day 5: Ghangaria to Govindghat

After breakfast, begin your descent towards Pulna, covering around 9-10 km downhill. From Pulna, take a jeep back to Govindghat, check into your hotel and relax after completing the trek.

Stay: Hotel in Govindghat  |  Meals: Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner
6

Day 6: Govindghat to Rishikesh

On the final day, after breakfast, begin your return journey to Rishikesh, retracing the scenic mountain route. The drive takes approximately 10-11 hours, bringing your journey to a close.

Meals: Breakfast

The Valley of Flowers Trek is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a breathtakingly beautiful trail in the Indian Himalayas. Located in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, this national park is renowned for its endemic alpine flowers and outstanding natural beauty. Combining this trek with the spiritual journey to Hemkund Sahib creates a holistic Himalayan experience that attracts travelers globally.

Standing at an altitude of approximately 14,400 feet (at Hemkund Sahib), the trek provides an incredible variety of scenery including dense forests, gushing rivers, magnificent waterfalls, and sweeping alpine meadows blooming with rare flora.

Best Time To Visit Valley of Flowers

The ideal season for the Valley of Flowers Trek is from July to September. During these monsoon months, the valley comes alive with thousands of blooming alpine flowers in vibrant colors. July and August offer maximum floral blossoms, while September provides clearer skies and slightly cooler temperatures.

Difficulty Level

The Valley of Flowers trek is classified as a moderate trek. The paths are well-defined and paved in many places, making it accessible for beginners. However, the steep ascent to Hemkund Sahib challenges stamina, so basic cardiovascular fitness and physical preparation are highly recommended.

Altitude

The valley itself lies around 11,500 to 12,500 feet. The highest point of the entire itinerary is Hemkund Sahib, which stands at an impressive 14,400 feet above sea level.

How To Reach

Trekkers usually begin the driving journey from Rishikesh or Haridwar. The base camp is Govindghat, accessible via a 10-11 hour scenic mountain drive alongside the Alaknanda river. The nearest airport is Jolly Grant in Dehradun.

Packing List

  • Headlamp

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Valley of Flowers suitable for beginners?

Yes. It is considered a perfect high-altitude trek for fit beginners due to the well-maintained trails.

How many days are required?

The full itinerary covering both the valley and Hemkund Sahib takes about 6 Days.

What is the maximum altitude?

Approximately 14,400 feet at Hemkund Sahib.

Can children join the trek?

Children above 10-12 years with good fitness can successfully participate.
