Day-Wise Itinerary

1

Day 1: Manali to Jobra — Trek to Chika Camp

Your adventure begins from Manali Mall Road at around 9:00 AM. After a scenic drive of approximately 22 km to Jobra, the trekking journey starts through beautiful pine forests, river crossings, and lush landscapes. The 4 km trek takes around 4-5 hours and gradually leads you to Chika Camp, situated at an altitude of 3,078 metres.

Meals: Lunch, Evening Snacks & Dinner  |  Stay: Alpine Camps
2

Day 2: Chika Camp to Balu Ka Ghera

After breakfast, begin the trek towards Balu Ka Ghera. Today's trail passes through expansive green meadows, glacier-fed streams, and breathtaking mountain landscapes. The trek covers approximately 8 km and takes around 4-5 hours to reach an altitude of 3,783 metres.

Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Snacks & Dinner
3

Day 3: Balu Ka Ghera to Shea Goru via Hampta Pass

Today marks the most exciting and challenging day of the trek. Ascent towards the magnificent Hampta Pass, situated at an altitude of 4,310 metres. From the top, witness the stunning transition from the lush green Kullu Valley to the barren and rugged landscapes of Spiti Valley. Descend towards Shea Goru Camp.

Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Snacks & Dinner
4

Day 4: Shea Goru to Chhatru — Excursion to Chandratal

Trek downhill towards Chhatru. From Chhatru, continue by vehicle towards the mesmerising Chandratal Lake, popularly known as the Moon Lake, located at an altitude of approximately 4,300 metres. After exploring the lake, return to Chhatru for an overnight stay.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks & Dinner
5

Day 5: Chhatru to Manali via Atal Tunnel

After breakfast, begin the return journey to Manali. The drive takes you through the world-famous Atal Tunnel. Enjoy the changing landscapes as you travel back from the rugged terrain of Lahaul to the lush valleys of Kullu. Reach Manali by approximately 3:00 PM.

Meals: Breakfast
