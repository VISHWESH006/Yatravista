Day-Wise Itinerary

0

Day 0: Delhi to Ransi Village

The journey begins from Delhi in the evening. Travelers will assemble at the designated pickup point and meet the Trip Captain for a brief orientation. After introductions and trip briefing, board the Tempo Traveller and begin the overnight journey towards the beautiful Garhwal Himalayas.

Stay: Overnight Journey
1

Day 1: Ransi Arrival & Local Exploration

Arrive in Rishikesh in the morning and continue the scenic drive towards Ransi Village. En route, visit the sacred Devprayag Sangam, where the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers merge to form the holy Ganga. Seek blessings at the famous Dhari Devi Temple. Check in to the homestay.

Meals: Dinner  |  Stay: Homestay at Ransi Village
2

Day 2: Trek to Madhmaheshwar Temple

Begin the scenic trek to the sacred Madhmaheshwar Temple, one of the revered Panch Kedar temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. The trail passes through dense forests, charming Himalayan villages, beautiful meadows, and flowing mountain streams. Attend the divine evening aarti.

Meals: Breakfast & Dinner  |  Stay: Guesthouse / Camps near Madhmaheshwar
3

Day 3: Budha Madhmaheshwar Visit & Return Journey

Early morning optional hike to Budha Madhmaheshwar for sunrise views over Chaukhamba and Kedarnath ranges. Return to the temple for breakfast and begin the descent trek back towards Ransi Village. Board the vehicle for the overnight drive back to Delhi.

Meals: Breakfast  |  Stay: Overnight Journey
