Day-Wise Itinerary

0

Day 0: Delhi to Yulla Khas Village

The journey begins from Delhi in the evening. Travelers will assemble at the designated pickup point and meet the Trip Captain for a short briefing about the trek and safety guidelines. Begin your overnight journey towards the beautiful Kinnaur region.

Stay: Overnight Travel
1

Day 1: Yulla Khas Village Arrival

After an early morning breakfast stop en route, continue your journey through the breathtaking Kinnaur-Tibet Highway alongside the Sutlej River. Arrive at Yulla Khas Village by evening, a picturesque settlement nestled in the mountains. Check-in at the homestay.

Meals: Dinner  |  Stay: Homestay at Yulla Khas
2

Day 2: Trek to Yulla Kanda Temple & Campsite Stay

Wake up early and begin the trek towards the sacred Yulla Kanda Temple, known as the World's Highest Krishna Temple. After reaching the temple at an altitude of 12,778 ft, seek blessings and admire the views of the Rora Valley and sacred lake. Descend to the campsite.

Meals: Breakfast & Dinner  |  Stay: Alpine Campsite
3

Day 3: Basecamp to Yulla Khas & Departure

Begin the day with breakfast and descend back to Yulla Khas Village. After freshening up and relaxing for some time, board the vehicle for the return journey to Delhi, arriving the following morning.

Meals: Breakfast  |  Stay: Overnight Travel
